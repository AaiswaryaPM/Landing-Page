Questions? We’ve got answers.

Is there a free plan for students?

Absolutely. Any student with a valid .edu email gets access to all Pro features for free for the first 12 months.

Can I invite external collaborators?

Yes, you can invite up to 5 guest users per project to view and comment on your work at no extra cost.

Is my data safe and private?

We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS in transit. Orbit is SOC2 Type II compliant, ensuring your data is handled with the highest security standards.

Does Orbit work offline?

Yes! You can continue writing notes and completing tasks without an internet connection. Your changes will automatically sync once you're back online.

Can I import data from Notion or Slack?

We offer a 1-click importer for Notion, Trello, and Slack. You can bring all your tasks and documents over in minutes without losing formatting.

What kind of AI features are included?

Orbit AI can summarize long document threads, turn meeting notes into actionable task lists, and even help you draft project outlines based on your team's previous work.
